# 评分标准(四项分别打分然后取平均得口语总分)

### 1. **Fluency and Coherence**

#### Fluency:

1. **Speech Rate**
2. **Speech continuity**

#### Coherence(连贯性)

1. **relevance**:
2. **Logical sequencing**:**逻辑连接词** 
3. **clear marking**:
4. **cohesive devices**:

$\textbf{\color{green}训练方法(不要背,关键词造句法):Focus 在语速和连贯性,多用逻辑连接词,逻辑连接词尽量多样,Write \& retell}$​

| Band | Rate&Length                          | Continuity                                                   | Coherence                                                    |
| ---- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 7    | Readily/without noticeable efforts   | Hesitation/repetition/self-correction won't affect coherence | 灵活使用逻辑连接词且Appropriate                              |
| 6    | a willingness to produce long terms  | Hesitation/repetition/self-correction may affect coherence   | 虽然不是对逻辑连接词的运用不是很恰当但是可以用a range of them |
| 5    | Usually able to keep going(能说就行) | Hesitations are associated with searches for basic language  | 只能用几个连接词还经常滥用                                   |
